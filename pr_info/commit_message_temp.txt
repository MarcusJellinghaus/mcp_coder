Refactor update_workflow_label to delegate to transition_issue_label

Replace the inline steps 6-9 (current-labels fetch, legacy idempotency
check, new-label-set computation, and set_labels call) with a single
delegation to the config-free transition_issue_label primitive from
LabelsMixin. Branch/issue resolution and labels.json lookup (steps 1-5)
remain in IssueManager; the public signature is unchanged.

Intentional narrow behavior change: stray workflow labels not equal to
the target are now stripped even when the target label is already
present (the primitive's stricter idempotency check). The 'Source label
... not present' INFO log is removed as a side-effect of delegating to
a primitive that has no concept of source vs clear-set labels.

Drop the now-obsolete log-assertion on
test_update_workflow_label_removes_different_workflow_label — the
set_labels call-args assertions on the same test already cover the
contract. All other existing tests in test_issue_manager_label_update.py
and test_issue_manager_labels.py pass unchanged, acting as regression
coverage.
